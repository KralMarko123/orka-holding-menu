          Board and Management

          Liljana Gajdovska - Executive Director

          Liljana Gajdoska was born in Skopje in 1974. In 1998 she graduated from the Faculty of Law at the Ss. Cyril and Methodius University in Skopje.

          Her professional activities and successful 25-year working career started in 1997, right here, in the family business, i.e. at one of the then few companies of the Kamchev family.

          Beginnings at the small trade company managed to awaken her sense of commitment, liability, honesty and fairness, values that the Kamchev management team knew to recognize and in 2013 appointed her as the director of one of the larger companies within the Holding - Kara AD Skopje Company that operated and owned real estate. Mutual respect and understanding have raised her to a managerial position at Panorama Residence Company in the period from 2013 to 2017; it is a company recognizable by its representative construction.

          Due to the fact, but above all her expertise, great experience, creativity, as well as strong determination and perseverance in achieving and implementing the goals set, as well as her positive human values, indisputable results and managerial skills presented as the head of the legal sector at the Company, the family recognized her as a person worthy of the highest position in the Company, and in October 2021 appointed her with full confidence as the CEO of Orka Holding.

          Kristina Kamcheva Stojcheska - Non-executive member of the Board of Directors

          Kristina Kamcheva Stojcheska was born in Skopje in 1972. In 1994 she completed her studies in Foreign Trade and Marketing at the Faculty of Economics at the Ss. Cyril and Methodius University in Skopje; then at the University of Tourism and Management Skopje – the Faculty of Economics she obtained a master's degree in economics.Following the theoretical upgrade, she became actively involved in the family businesses of her father, Ilija Kamchev. Since then, she has been directly involved in the management of the most companies and projects within Orka Holding.

          Her education continued with the Northwestern University Certifications - Organizational Leadership Specialization - Leadership through Design Innovation - High Performance Collaboration: Leadership, Teamwork and Negotiation - Leadership through Social Influence - Leadership Communication for Maximum Impact - Leadership throughMarketing - Initiating and Planning Projects.

          Her work has been particularly recognized in the textile industry. Using her expertise, the domestic textile brand Kara has been created, practically as an example of a highly professional clothing line launched in the country. She has been directly involved in creating the production assortment, marketing and overall organization of the production process.

          In the period from 2017 to 2019, she has been appointed to the position of General Manager of Mlekara Zdravje Radovo, where she has faced the challenge of restructuring the overall production process, rebranding products, organizing sales and marketing. Her engagement has had visible results in reducing the unproductive costs, raising the effectiveness of the production process and raising the brand's recognition in the domestic market. In addition, Kamcheva Stojcheska has been personally involved in launching the IBIS hotel brands as part of the AKOR Group in Macedonia. She was the initiator of introducing the developed world franchise, primarily by opening the IBIS Hotel Skopje City Center and then the IBIS Styles.

          Moreover, she has an active role in Ilija Kamchev Foundation, established in honour of Ilija Kamchev, the founder of Orka Holding. As a person who adores art and humanity, she is a proud supporter of young people and established artists from Macedonia as well, being at the same time an organizer of many donor activities for marginalized categories of citizens.

          Vanche Mijalchev - Non-executive member of the Board of Directors

          Vanche Mijalchev was born in Probistip in 1971. He graduated from the Faculty of Electrical Engineering, section Computer Engineering, Informatics and Automation at the Ss. Cyril and Methodius University in Skopje.

          His professional experience started in 1994 when he worked as a professor in Informatics at the high school in Probishtip.

          At the local elections in 1996 he was elected mayor, a position that enabled him to lead the municipality of Probishtip successfully until the end of his term in 2000.

          In 1999 he became member of the management team of Rudnici Zletovo as Chairman of the Supervisory Board up to 2002, and in 2001 he was appointed by the Government of the Republic of North Macedonia as Executive Director of PE HS Zletovica; he successfully performed that position until 2003.

          In the period from 2004 to 2010 he was more professionally focused in the field of engineering and informatics as a director of his own IT company, and then as a director of the private Institute for Information Research. Afterwards he was further engaged at the Academy for Judges and Public Prosecutors at the position of system administrator until 2013.

          Since 2013 onwards he has completely dedicated to the management in the field of construction, initially at Beton Shtip GTD AD Shtip at the position of executive member of the Board of Directors, and then at other companies in the field of construction; currently he is the director at GTD Beton Shtip DOO Shtip.

          Considering his many years of managerial experience and knowledge he has become member of the Orka Holding team where currently he is non-executive member of the Board of Directors.
