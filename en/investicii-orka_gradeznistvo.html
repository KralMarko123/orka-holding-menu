          ORKA Construction

          Beton Shtip

          The construction trade company Beton Shtip AD Shtip is a joint stock company that has successfully existed for half a century. Beton Shtip designs and constructs buildings of the highest quality in all fields in construction.

          Employees at Beton Shtip have participated in the construction of our country, including industrial, residential buildings, schools, hospitals, bridges, dams and many other buildings.

          Following its growth of the domestic market, Beton Shtip started to undertake even the most complex construction works abroad including: Czech Republic, Slovakia, Germany, Iraq, Libya, Bulgaria, Latvia, Ukraine, Russia, Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan.
