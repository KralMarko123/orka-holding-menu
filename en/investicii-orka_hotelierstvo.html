          ORKA Hotel Industry and Catering Industry

          Hotel Ibis Skopje City Center

          Hotel Ibis Skopje City Center is located downtown, close to administrative institutions and major tourist attractions, making it attractive for both business guests and tourists.

          The hotel has 110 fully equipped rooms, a modern bar and a web corner. Comfortable and pleasant sleep, varied breakfast menus and numerous discounts for the loyal guests of the Ibis family make the hotel the first choice of guests visiting Skopje. Ibis hotels guarantee and provide unique services all over the world.

          Ibis employees offer the best service and meet the requirements of every guest, which is confirmed by the good reputation and numerous awards and recognitions by numerous institutions and organizations.

          The Ibis brand is part of the Accor Group’s hotel chain and is present in 48 countries with more than 1,800 hotels. Thanks to the success of this concept, Ibis hotels have proven to be the European market leader.

          Hotel Ibis Styles Skopje

          Guided by the idea of creating a new place in the city characteristic of its unique and refined style, in 2013 Ibis Styles Hotel was established. Uniquely equipped, with a capacity of 47 rooms, Ibis Styles is located in a quiet city district, 2.8 km from the center of Skopje.

          The hotel, where all rooms have a modern and elegant design, provides an atmosphere and conditions for a pleasant stay and accommodation. The design of Ibis Styles integrates a number of modern amenities, such as a modern lobby bar and free Wi-Fi internet. All rooms at the hotel have a modern and elegant design, with high quality sleeping mattresses and a modern comfortable bathroom.

          The hotel has a meeting room with a capacity for 45 people, equipped with LCD projector and TV, flipchart boards and other technical equipment for holding presentations. Ibis Styles offers 24-hour reception service, as well as laundry and dry cleaning services, luggage storage and video surveillance.

          The hotel has excellent connections to the city’s shopping malls, as well as to banks, economic and cultural institutions, museums and monuments.

          SushiCo

          Internationally renowned brand SushiCo is a chain of restaurants with typical Japanese, Thai and Chinese found in many places around the world. Opening the restaurant in Skopje, which is part of this international franchise, the SushiCo brand announced its presence in the Balkans too. The restaurant has a sophisticated design offering the visitors a maximum enjoyment and a pleasant atmosphere.

          Located in the heart of the city, in Debar Maalo, SushiCo is the first sushi restaurant in the country providing exceptional high-quality specialties from the modern cuisines of Japan, Thailand and China. In the process of food preparation, broad attention is paid to compliance with quality standards. SushiCo restaurant is recognizable by the highly professional service of its staff that is an additional motive for visiting it on various occasions. The restaurant is a great place for family or business meetings or gatherings with friends, and it provides free food delivery to the desired location in Skopje as well.

          The exceptional service and delicious menu are the main reason why Sushico restaurants are the first choice of people who love different tastes of the Far East. In order to achieve the original look of the Asian restaurants, every detail of the interior is designed as a recognizable feature of the Eastern tradition with a modern interpretation, while the SushiCo’s kitchen employs professional chefs from Japan, China and Thailand.

          OK Restaurants & Bars

          OK Restaurants & Bars is an internal restaurant and cafeteria for Acibadem Sistina’s staff. It has operated since the opening of the Hospital, enlarging its spatial capacities and expanding itsmenu over the years. Today it covers an area of 255 square meters, indoor space and a terrace, and has a capacity for 120 people.

          The restaurant has a modern and unique design so that employees can have their daily meal or coffee break in a pleasant and relaxed atmosphere. The kitchen of OK Restaurants & Bars is equipped and operates in accordance with the most modern standards, always offering delicious and freshly prepared food for the hospital staff. There are over 40 different types of food on its menu, including hot and cold drinks.

          In addition to its standard services, the restaurant organizes cocktail promotions for the needs of Acibadem Sistina Hospital as well.
